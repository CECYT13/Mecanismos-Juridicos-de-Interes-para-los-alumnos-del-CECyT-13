Unida de aprendizaje: INGLES.

In this learning unit, we coordinated the presentation of the classroom project in a foreign language. The project focused on publicizing and providing information about the process of reviewing a qualification to help the students of CECyT No. 13.

Using a flow chart, we detailed the steps required to request this review mechanism, ensuring that students understand the process and can utilize it effectively.
